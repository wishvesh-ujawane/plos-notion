## Extra properties (add beyond WORKSPACE-DESIGN.md)

### Cohorts — add
- **Duration (weeks)** — formula: `if(empty(prop("Start Date")) or empty(prop("End Date")), "", format(round(dateBetween(prop("End Date"), prop("Start Date"), "days") / 7)) + "w")`
- **Days to Start** — formula: `if(empty(prop("Start Date")), "", if(dateBetween(prop("Start Date"), now(), "days") < 0, "started", format(dateBetween(prop("Start Date"), now(), "days")) + "d"))`
- **Active Students** — rollup of `Students → Status` → count where `Status` in [`Enrolled`, `Active`]
- **Expenses** — rollup of Cloud101 Finance where `Kind` = `Expense` → sum of `Amount`
- **Net** — formula: `prop("Revenue") - prop("Expenses")`
- **Sessions Held** — rollup of Sessions → count all

### Students — add
- **Days Since Enrolled** — formula: `if(empty(prop("Enrolled Date")), "", format(dateBetween(now(), prop("Enrolled Date"), "days")) + "d")`
- **Payments** — rollup of Cloud101 Finance (via Student relation) → sum of `Amount` where `Kind` = `Revenue`
- **At Risk** — formula: `if(prop("Status") == "Active" and prop("Progress %") < 30 and dateBetween(now(), prop("Enrolled Date"), "days") > 21, "⚠️", "")`
- **Progress Bar** — formula → [00-conventions.md § 6.4](00-conventions.md#64-progress-emoji-bar)

### Curriculum — add
- **Sessions Using** — rollup of Sessions → count all (via `Module` relation)
- **Cohorts Count** — rollup of `Cohorts` relation → count all

### Sessions — add
- **Attendance %** — formula: `if(empty(prop("Attendance")), "", format(round(prop("Attendance") / prop("Cohort").prop("Active Students") * 100)) + "%")`
  - _(If the nested-rollup pattern isn't supported cleanly, drop this and compute manually per session.)_
- **Days Ago** — formula: `if(empty(prop("Date")), "", format(dateBetween(now(), prop("Date"), "days")) + "d ago")`

### Business Tasks — add
- **Today** — checkbox
- **Priority Score** — formula → [00-conventions.md § 6.2](00-conventions.md#62-priority-score)
- **Days Until Due** — formula → [00-conventions.md § 6.1](00-conventions.md#61-days-until-due)

### Cloud101 Finance — add
- **Month** — formula: `formatDate(prop("Date"), "YYYY-MM")` (for grouping by month in views)
- **Signed Amount** — formula: `if(prop("Kind") == "Expense", -1 * prop("Amount"), prop("Amount"))` (so a single sum rollup gives net)
